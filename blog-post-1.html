This is my first time ever writing a blog post!

March 20, 2020

share on:

Image credits to Unsplash.com

So, where do I start? Yes, this is my first time writing on a blog. But it's definitely no the first one I see.

I might seem overconfident, but the truth is I'm willing to fail.

Yes! That's exactly what I said.

People are afraid of failing. That's the one reason why not everyone succeeds. Success requires a little failing too and I'm willing to dive into it.

I can accept failure, everyone fails at something. But I can't accept not trying. - Michael Jordan

Will my first blog post be about success and failure? Too cliché for my taste, but I already started.

I guess what I'm trying to say is, what I REALLY want is my 1st blog post ever to be meaningful somehow!

So, food for thought. What are you NOT failing at because you're not willing to try?

The real failure is when you stop trying...or even worse, when you didn't even try at all!
