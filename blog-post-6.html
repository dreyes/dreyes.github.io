This is by far, my favorite productivity tool!

March 20, 2020

share on:

Image credits to Unsplash.com

I've tried so many tools out there, from note-taking apps to to-do lists, reminders, calendars, and others.

Nothing can beat what Trello can do! This has to be the most impactful tool in my swiss army knife.

Mix a whiteboard with post-its and an office assistant, what do you get? Trello!

Its ease of use and amicable environment, and the lean/kanban approach on its boards, make it shine above the rest.

No! I'm not getting paid to say this! So why I'm I so enthusiastic to even make a post about it?

Simple, it makes my day better! It's easy to organize everything I do, from planning a trip to organizing my workload. It does everything.

And I left the best for last. Trello, with its somewhat, recently acquired Butler, gets it on steroids!

Butler makes automation native, and for a tech enthusiast like me, it can't get any better!
